About us

What are amigurumis?

Read more

Amigurumi is the Japanese craft of crocheting small stuffed toys, such as animals, play food, or dolls.

While the art of amigurumi has been known in Japan for several decades, the craft first started appealing to the masses in other countries, especially in the West, in 2003.

Amigurumi may be used as children's toys but are generally purchased or made solely for aesthetic purposes.
